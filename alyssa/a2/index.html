Simple Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe

Introduction

A simple yet delicious strawberry banana smoothie recipe that everyone loves. All it takes is 4 ingredients, a blender, and a few minutes to whip it up! This recipe was inspired by Lisa Bryan's recipe here.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup of milk
  • ½ cup of Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Peel, slice, and then freeze a banana at -18°C for 1 hour
  2. Put all your ingredients in the blender
  3. Blend until smooth
