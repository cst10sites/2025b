SCP-244

About

Item #: SCP-244

Object Class: Thaumiel

Description: SCP-244 appears as a 27-year-old human male, seemingly of Greek descent. He is consistently wearing a face mask, although he has been reported to have privately shown the O5 Council his face ██ times. Aside from his clothes and accessories, he does not appear different compared to others, and his anomalous properties reside within him. Due to a request regarding privacy concerns, the O5 Council has decided to keep his exact appearance details anonymous.

Anomalous Properties

Figure A: Due to a direct request from SCP-244, we have opted to not show his face.

SCP-244 manifests whenever a random individual is in a difficult situation; whether it's issues with life, a lethal fight, or just any other type of difficult situation, SCP-244 will appear (although his manifesting/teleportation is done only when nothing is watching). As soon as he is there, SCP-244 will resolve the situation; if the individual is dealing with a financial situation, SCP-244 will give them tips, sometimes slipping information of the future, and can also give the individual a sum of money. If the individual is coping with the death of a loved one, SCP-244 will console them and accompany them until they feel better; SCP-244 is also said to have a soothing effect. If the individual is in the process of being assaulted, SCP-244 will come to the rescue and fight off the assailants (though he never kills) before comforting the individual and treating their wounds. No matter how extreme the situation is, SCP-244 will always find a way to protect the individual from every source of harm. In short, SCP-244 will find a way to help and even potentially rescue individuals from difficult situations.

SCP-244 has been shown to be invincible, as some individuals have reported him being able to survive onslaughts of bullets. Despite this, he is shown bleeding, although he seems to contain an infinite amount of blood. His clothes are also as durable as him, never being destroyed and always reappearing on him after a short period of time. Despite being invincible, SCP-244 is not immortal, as in the years, he has been shown to have aged (although it's not known if he can die of old age). He is very physically strong, being able to beat many at once with ease. On top of his excellent physical power, SCP-244 is also very intelligent; he can solve complex puzzles quickly and is capable of talking down all suicidal individuals he has encountered from harming themselves. Personality-wise, he is usually as optimistic as he is while talking to troubled individuals. It takes great effort to even alter the genuine and unwavering smile on his face. To show his goodwill, he took the time to make a brief and friendly interactive quiz, attached at the bottom of this page.

Fighting Feats

SCP-244 is a tremendously strong fighter. He does not have a reported strength limit, as he has once been able to lift a skyscraper to rescue the people inside. His fighting approach resembles that of a martial arts master. He has been pitted against several strong SCPs, including SCP-096, 173, and 682. When approaching SCP-096, SCP-244 made sure to not look at his face and was able to engage in a peaceful meditation activity with SCP-096. When a D-class saw an image of SCP-096's face, SCP-244 was able to restrain it from attacking and succeeded in doing so, calming it down in the process. In his encounter with SCP-173, SCP-244 survived the attack, although he did not fight back or attempt to communicate, deeming SCP-173 too lacking in sapience. When SCP-173 tried to attack a guard, however, SCP-244 came to his defense and held SCP-173 back, allowing other guards to arrive and back the guard up. And in a brief battle against SCP-682, SCP-244 convinced the monster of the goodness of humanity and life and was eventually able to convince it to calm down. From that point, SCP-682 became passive (until it was eventually put in a test against SCP-███ and reverted to its original state). Due to these feats, SCP-244 has been reclassified from Keter class to Thaumiel. When asked about helping "rescue" more violent SCPs ("lost souls" according to SCP-244), he happily agreed. As of today, several experiments involving SCP-244 and destructive SCPs have been scheduled.
