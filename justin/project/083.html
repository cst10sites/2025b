SCP-083

About

Item #: SCP-053

Object Class: Euclid

Description: SCP-083 is a dated labor camp found in an abandoned field in ████████, Russia. It appears reminiscent of a smaller Gulag, made near the time of the Stalinist purges (1934-38). SCP-083 appears to be very barren, with no signs of plant life on the ground. It is also nearly empty, only containing a few items like tools, although there are a few structures, including the watchtower seen next to the cabin.

Due to its anomalous nature, this description applies to the "true" state of SCP-083 when it is observed while near.

Anomalous Properties

Figure A: Image taken of SCP-083 from an unknown Russian explorer.

When SCP-083 is perceived closely, one can observe it in its "true" state. It is shown as an abandoned gulag containing no signs of life and is nearly devoid of items and tools. However, from afar, it is shown as a raging battlefield. Many soldiers and prisoners (SCP-083-I) can be seen fighting, although it is difficult to perceive any details of the fight. Some corpses can be seen, with blood splattered on some parts of the Gulag. A few militaristic weapons, like tanks and rocket launchers, can also be observed. Sound is also heard, as noted by explorers who heard yelling and explosions from the battlefield. The factions are unknown, as all fighters are dressed up differently.

Even if one enters SCP-083, they will still be unable to see or interact with anything shown from the mirage. Any physical interactions or false projectiles launched will pass through their bodies. In some instances, the fighters may attempt to interact directly with the explorer either by attempting to save them or by attacking them, although both attempts will fail and the observer very soon becomes ignored, making it difficult to have any complex interactions with SCP-083-I.

SCP-083-I

SCP-083-I are human apparitions shown by SCP-083. They are shown fighting with each other. On average, they seem to appear as 6-foot-tall humans of Soviet descent with clothes representing the era (although none of them adhere to any specific soldier uniforms). They do not seem to have the ability to communicate vocally aside from gibberish and shouting. While their bodies appear to be 100% human, their clothes are distorted versions of Russian uniforms in the 20th century. Up until incident 083-B, they didn't show any signs of sentience, solely fighting each other on the battlefield for an infinite amount of time. They don't appear to be able to take any damage, as some can be observed surviving shell rounds from the tanks and missile launchers. And as with their unsuccessful interactions with observers wandering in the Gulag, any real, non-mirage projectiles fired also phase through the fighters, although they do seem to notice the weapons and try avoiding them, mistaking them for a false missile. This property extends to the corpses, who are also unaffected by any physical damage.

Incident 083-B


<Begin Log>

Seargeant K: Corporal! Can you hear me now?

Corporal ███████: Seargeant! You're back! I'll tell you what we've been seing. So far, the fighters all seem to be ignoring you. Some of them tried to push you but they couldn't touch you. So, most of them have given up already.

Seargeant K: Thanks for the report, Seargeant. I'll stay here for a bit longer. Report to me if anything changes...

[The loud shouting, hitting, and firing sounds can still be heard]

Seargeant K: I'm going to try and hit them. I'll throw a few kicks and punches.

Corporal ███████: It appears that they do not feel your attacks, like how you don't feel theirs.

[Loud grunting and smashing sounds can still be heard, followed by sounds of multiple gunshots.]

Seargeant K: Seems like we are unable to interact with them, then. I'll stay here for a bit longer, then I'll rejoin you at the station to make observations myself.

Corporal ███████: Sounds good, Seargeant.

Seargeant K: Hold on... I have an idea, corporal! Try producing a lot of sound and see if they react.

Corporal ███████: Alright, Seargeant! I will try that with my megaphone. Everyone, put on your earmuffs.

[The megaphone can be heard being turned on.]

Corporal ███████: HELLO? CAN YOU HEAR ME?

[Suddenly, all of the fighters stop. They turn to look at Corporal ███████ and his men.]

[They begin quickly sprinting towards him, exiting the gulag.]

Corporal ███████: Oh shoot! Everyone! Stop looking at them! Cover your eyes!

<End Log>


Aftermath

Ever since Incident 083-B, the gulag and its surroundings have been enclosed in walls of thick iron coatings. The object class has been changed from Safe to Euclid, as the apparitions have been shown to be capable of exiting the gulag, and the danger they pose is unknown. No researcher is to ever observe it again without direct permission from the O5 Council.
