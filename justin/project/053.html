SCP-053

About

Item #: SCP-053

Object Class: Safe

Description: SCP-053 appears to be an old wooden closet. It is found in a similarly old wooden cottage (SCP-053-I), and it dates from approximately the early 20th century. SCP-053 appears to be very hollow, with no shelves. It is also empty, containing no items, although there is a lot of dust.

Anomalous Properties

Figure A: Still frame taken from video footage of Exploration I

As soon as someone enters SCP-053-I, SCP-053 releases a smoky odor (directly from the closet, although the exact source is unknown), although it emits no smoke. This substance is similar to chloroform, although its anomalous property allows its effect to penetrate any nasal protection, and it will soon render the human unconscious regardless of whether they have a gas mask or not. Upon regaining consciousness, one will find themself inside SCP-053-I again, but in a different spot. The interiors of SCP-053-I are also changed, with the windows blurred and the door gone. Entering SCP-053 directly will simply lead to another instance of SCP-053-I. After approximately 10 actual minutes, the odor will return and knock out any humans in the room, and it will send them back to SCP-053-I but now in the real world again. However, after approximately 5 minutes after entering the realm, any connections to the real world will be destroyed, and time gets slowed down, with an hour in this world being less than a second in the real world.

At this point, entering the closet will no longer lead one to the exact room; small deviations will start occurring. These deviations are based on how the observer is interpreting SCP-053-I. Thus, any memory loss of certain parts of the room or any misremembering will cause the room to alter its appearance and properties. The rooms should be addressed as Mental Stages. As time passes, this effect becomes more volatile, to a point where it is no longer recommended to enter the closet, and one should rather stay as far away as possible from it. Some wanderers have stated that there is a possibility for entities to spawn should they imagine one, and these entities can be lethal and ██████████ ███ ███████. These entities can also spawn further down the realm should one eventually succumb to insanity, and they are capable of crossing through Mental Stages through closets to hunt down and chase the player in a different or even earlier Mental State (room). Therefore, it is highly recommended that one sleep as soon as they are transported to the world.

SCP-053-I

SCP-053-I appears to be an old and abandoned one-story-tall cottage cabin in ██████████, Canada. The bricks are very dusty and observably old. It is quite small in size. It has only one window and a door shown on the outside. Aside from the closet, it contains a dusty bed. The door does not include any anomalous properties and functions properly, with door hinges and a lock. However, the window is seemingly permanently blurred due to the unremovable dirt attached to it despite attempts to clean it and never shows a clear view of the inside. Inside the room, there is a single bright, buzzing, fluorescent lightbulb. All those features remain unchanged even when the observer is teleported to the infinite dimension. Until a termination request is due, the durability of the structure is unknown.
