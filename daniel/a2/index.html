My Recipe: Mac and Cheese

Introduction

Mac and Cheese is my favorite comfort food because it is creamy, cheesy, and delicious. It reminds me of family gatherings and cozy nights in. Here is an image of my favorite Mac and Cheese dish:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Boil ½ cup of water to 100°C.
  2. Add the elbow macaroni and cook until tender (about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Drain the macaroni and return it to the pot.
  4. Add the butter, milk, and shredded cheddar cheese to the pot.
  5. Stir over low heat until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.
  6. Season with salt and serve hot.
