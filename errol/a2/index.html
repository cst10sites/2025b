Breakfast Burrito

Introduction

This is my favourite meal to have in the morning because it tastes like a whole breakfast in one meal. It is also healthy for you and high in protein. Overall, its just a great way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Three Eggs
  • Two pieces of Bacon
  • One tortilla wrap
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

Step 1

Firstly, you will want to crack 3 eggs and pour it into a place to mix.

Once in an area to mix you will stir the eggs around till its all one liqued.

Step 2

After the eggs are mixed you will want to cook them, which you can do by putting a frying pan on an oven and setting the heat to medium (70°C).

(I recommend you put some oil or butter on the pan. This will help you cook the eggs easier.)

Now put the mixed eggs in the pan and wait for it to cook. While waiting you should move the egg yolk around with a spatula, making sure all the eggs are being cooked. It should be cooked after 10 minutes on medium heat. (63°C)

Now that your eggs are cooked you will put them onto a tortilla. Make sure it is in a long pile in the middle.

Step 3

To make a Breakfast Burrito you will need two pieces of bacon. To cook bacon, you should leave the heat on medium and leave it on each side for about 4-5 minutes, until its golden brown.

When cooked place the two pieces of bacon on top of the pile of eggs.

Step 4

Now that you have cooked the eggs and the bacon, you will add as much salt and pepper as you'd like. Finally fold the tortilla to complete the Burrito.

To fold it, take the tortilla and take it over top of the ingredients, and tuck it underneath. Take the side and fold it inside towards the ingredients. To finish it, you roll the tortilla with the ingredients over the rest of the tortilla wrap until you just have a cylinder.
