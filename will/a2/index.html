How to make Annie's Mac and Cheese

Introduction

Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 10 minutes Total time: 15 minutes

Mac and cheese has been around since the 13th century and has been enjoyed by millions. It is the ultimate comfort food, and there is no better way to make it than Annie's. There is just something about the soft macaroni and the warm cheese that, when prepared correctly, cannot be defeated. It feels too good to believe that this delight can be fully prepared in only ¼ of an hour! Personally, Annie's mac and cheese is best enjoyed in the comfort of your home, eating with your loved ones. For me, I enjoy spicing up my mac and cheese with bacon bits and hot sauce, though it is not necessary. Whatever floats your boat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Annie's macoroni
  • 1 Annie's cheese packet
  • 3 Tbs milk

Instructions

  1. Fill a medium saucepan with 6 cups of water and bring to a boil, 100°C over high heat.
  2. Pour in the pasta and let cook for around 8-10 minutes, stirring.
  3. Pour the pasta with water into a colander, then back into the saucepan with 3 Tbs of milk, stirring in the cheese powder.
  4. Stir until everything is combined
  5. ENJOY!
