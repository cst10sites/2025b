What Is the Clown Olympics?

Clown Olympics is an invented yet humorous tournament that honors comedy, creativity, and clowning from all over the world. Events consist of activities such as unicycle races, juggling marathons, and a good deal of custard pie throwing.

Clowning festivals are also held in Mexico under the name of "Festival Internacional del Payaso." International clowns come to present comedic styles peculiar to their own culture.
