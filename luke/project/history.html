A Brief History

The clown Olympics started out as a gathering of performers in the early '80s. Over time, it was indeed altered by Boston into an international event, combining traditional clowning with theatrical performance. Mexico's impact was especially great; they hosted the 1998 games and then other countries began to follow suit.

Current Champion

David Larible continues to be an icon in clowning, blending physical comedy with heartfelt performance.
