OH or one handed is just solving a 3x3 with one hand. Most people use their non-dominant hand. It's very similar to regular 3x3, however if you don't learn good fingertricks your times will be very slow.



This video will help you improve at OH. It covers wide moves, look ahead and how to reduce rotations.
