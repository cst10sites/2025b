It is common for people to learn redux first for 4x4 however I would recommend learning Yao first as it is better and it is hard to transition from redux to Yao. Yao solves to first 2 centers and cross edges first followed by the next for centers then the rest of the edges until you reach 3x3 stage. If you haven’t already, learn how to solve a 3x3 first.
