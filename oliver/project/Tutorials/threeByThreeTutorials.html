I find this to be the best tutorial available for learning f2l (first 2 layers). This is the most important step in improving after you get good and consistent at beginners’ method.



Beginner oll (orientation of the last layer) and pll (permutation of the last layer) solves the last layer in 4 steps (2 for pll and 2 for oll). I find it easier to learn these from a video mainly because its most likely the first algs you must memorize and are easy to visualize especially for oll because it's mostly taking a pair out and re-inserting it differently. Although if you prefer to use an alg sheet go see 2look pll and 2look oll under Algs



I recommend learning the algs themselves on a alg sheet (see algs, full pll and full oll) however learn the fingertricks from the videos above. In total there are 78 algs and you should not learn more than 1-3 a day so you don't forget them so it can take a while. These two alg sets will solve the last layer in 2 algs.
