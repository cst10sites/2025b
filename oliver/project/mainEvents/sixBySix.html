Overview:

6x6 was added to the WCA in 2009. It was invented in 2004 by Panagiotis Verdes. The current world record average of 5 is held by Max Park of the United States of America set in April 2024 with an average of 1:39.68 minutes. The world record single is held by Max Park of the United States of America set in July 2024 with a time of 1:34.15 minutes. (as of June 11th, 2025)
