Overview:

Megaminx was an original event in the WCA. It was invented in 1982 by Uwe Meffert. The current world record average of 5 is held by Timofei Tarasenko of Russia set in February 2025 with an average of 25.38 seconds. The world record single is held by Aidan Grainger of the United Kingdom set in May 2025 with a time of 22.89 seconds. (as of June 13th, 2025)
