Overview:

4x4 was one of the original events of the WCA. It was invented in 1981 by Péter Sebestény. The current world record average of 5 is held by Tymon Kolasiński of Poland set in February 2025 with an average of 19.17 seconds. The world record single is held by Max Park of The United States of America in June 2024 with a time of 15.71 seconds. (as of June 10th, 2025)
