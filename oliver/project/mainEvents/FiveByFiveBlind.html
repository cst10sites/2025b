Overview:

5x5 blindfolded was one of the original events of the WCA. The current world record average of 3 is held by Stanley Chapel of the United States of America set in December 2019 with an average of 2:27.63 minutes. The world record single is held by Stanley Chapel of The United States of America in March 2025 with a time of 2:03.33 minutes. (as of June 16th, 2025)

Best 5x5's of 2024/2025

Flagship:

GAN562 M 5x5 (UV Coated or Matte)

  • Extremely stable and fast turning
  • Very resistant to locks and pops
  • Used for all recent world records
  • $93.06/80.75

MoYu AoChuang V6 5x5 (Triple-Track + UV)

  • Can be easier to set up and break in then the Gan
  • Very consistent
  • $84.85

Mid-range:

MoYu AoChuang V6 5x5 (Double-Track + UV)

  • Amazing outer layers and overall performance
  • great if you prefer smaller puzzles
  • $75.27

Vin Cube 5x5 (Ball-Core + UV)

  • Nice and controlable outer layers
  • In between size of Gan and AoChuang
  • Good combination for both speed solving and blind solving
  • $47.89

Budget:

MoYu AoChuang V6 5x5 (Single-Track)

  • Best performance you will get for a budget 5x5
  • feels very similar to the other AoChuang's
  • $31.47

MFJS MeiLong 5x5 M

  • Good if you just want a magnetic 5x5 and don't care to much about speedsolving
  • Best 5x5 at its price
  • $17.76
