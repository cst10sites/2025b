Overview:

Clock was an original member of the WCA. It was invented in 1988 by Christopher C. Wiggs. The current world record average of 5 is held by Lachlan Gibson of New Zealand set in March 2025 with an average of 2.28 seconds. The world record single is held by Volodymyr Kapustianskyi of the United States of America set in May 2025 with a time of 1.64 seconds. (as of June 19th, 2025)
