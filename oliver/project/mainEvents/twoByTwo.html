Overview:

2x2 was one of the original events of the WCA. It was invented in 1970 by Larry Nichols. The current world record average of 5 is held by Yiheng Wang of China set in December 2024 with an average of 0.88 seconds. The world record single is held by Teodor Zajder of Poland set in November 2023 with a time of 0.43 seconds. (as of June 10th, 2025)
