Overview:

Skewb was added to the WCA in 2014. It was invented in 1982 by Tony Durham. The current world record average of 5 is held by Ignacy Samselski of Poland set in June 2025 with an average of 1.37 seconds. The world record single is held by Carter Kucala of the United States of America set in March 2024 with a time of 0.75 seconds. (as of June 16th, 2025)
