Overview:

Square-1 was an original member of the WCA. It was invented in 1990 by Karel Hršel and Vojtěch Kopský. The current world record average of 5 is held by Sameer Aggarwal of the United States of America set in February 2025 with an average of 4.63 seconds. The world record single is held by Ryan Pilat of the United States of America set in March 2024 with a time of 3.41 seconds. (as of June 16th, 2025)
