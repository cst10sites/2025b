Overview:

Pyraminx was an original member of the WCA. It was invented in 1970 by Uwe Mèffert. The current world record average of 5 is held by Sebastian Lee of Australia set in November 2024 with an average of 1.15 seconds. The world record single is held by Simon Kellum of the United States of America set in December 2023 with a time of 0.73 seconds. (as of June 13th, 2025)
