Overview:

Multi-Blind was an original member of the WCA. In multi blind you get one hour to solve as many cubes blindfolded you can in a row. The hour includes memorizing each cube then solving them. The current world record is 63/66 cubes solved by Rowe Hessler in April 2025(as of June 4th, 2025)
