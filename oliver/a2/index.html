White chocolate cashew cookies

Introduction

White chocolate cashew cookies are definitely my favorite type of baked good and probably in my top ten foods. My preferred way to eat it is microwave it for 15 seconds to make it warm and chewy, almost like it just came out of the oven. A more popular version of this cookie is white chocolate macedamia nut however I think cashews taste the same in a cookie if not better and are easier to find at grocery stores. I don't have a specific recipe I usually use but for this one I am using this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • ½teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of granultaed sugar
  • 1 cup of brown suagr
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1½ of white chocolate chips
  • ⅔ cup of cashews

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and very lightly grease backing sheat or line with parchment or baking mat.
  2. In a medium bowl wisk together, flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  3. In a large bowl cream together butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar.
  4. Mix in eggs and vanilla to large bowl.
  5. Mix the wet and dry ingredients together just until combined.
  6. Stir in white cochoclate chips and cashews.
  7. Use a cookie scoop to scoop dough into mounds and arrange 2-3 inches apart on your prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes until cookies start to brown around the edges.
  9. Cool for 5-10 minutes on the pan before transfering onto wire rack to cool completly.
