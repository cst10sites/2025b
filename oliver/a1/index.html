A bit about myself

Hello, my name is Oliver. Last year if you were to ask me this same question you would get a different answer, not super different but different. Last year I would have been able to say only one thing about myself really, and that is that I really like soccer. And I still do, it's still my favorite thing to talk about and watch and do. However, since summer I've been spending most of my free time on a different hobby, speedcubing. Speedcubing, hence the name is solving a Rubik cube in as little time as possible. Right now, my best time is 8.83 seconds, and I have done over 25000 solves. There are many reasons why I love this so much, the first one being the data. I can't really explain why I like data so much but it's just fun to look at it. Another reason why I like it a lot is because you can see your progress through the data, and I know how much effort I put in even if I have just improved by a tenth of a second. One of the most useful resources for me to do this is jperm.net. This website helped me memorize and practice 58 algorithms necessary to know the most popular method.
