My Recipe of Chicken Alfredo

Introduction

The chicken alfredo is one of the most popular type alfredos and a staple in italine cusines. I picked this food for random reason becuase at the time it was in my mind.

Ingredients

The main ingridients needed are:

  • Chicken
  • Pasta(commonly Fettuccine)
  • Alfredo Sauce(butter,heavy whip cream, grated parmesean cheese,garlic,other seasoning)

Instruction

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook The Chicken:Season the chicken, pan-fry in oil and butter for 6 minute a side, or until an internal temperature reaches 165°F. Put it on a cutting and let it rest then cut it in to ½. Tent with foil, while you prepare the sause
  3. Make the Alfredo sause:Using the same pan you used to cook the chicken,the cream,butter and seasoning are used and simmered on medium-low heat until thickened,then the Parmesan is added in until melted and smooth.
  4. Assemble:Drain the pasta,reserving some of the liquid to loosen the sauce.Toss immediately with the Alfredo sauce. Divide the pasta among serving bowl and top with slices of the cooked chicken. Garnish with parsley, more Parmesan, and black pepper if desired.
