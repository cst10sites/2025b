Beginners boxing gear guide

What you need to start:


Boxing Gloves (12–16 oz)

Gloves protect your hands and wrists when hitting the heavy bag, mitts, or during sparring. Without gloves, you risk spraining your wrist or breaking your knuckles.

Recommended Beginner Gloves

Hand Wraps

Hand wraps add another layer of protection inside the gloves. They keep your wrists stable and stop your knuckles from getting bruised.

Recommended Hand Wraps

Mouthguard

A mouthguard protects your teeth and reduces shock to your jaw during sparring or accidental contact. Even light contact can chip a tooth or cause jaw pain.

Recommended Mouthguard

Boxing Bag

Train from home with this hanging punching bag where you can practice combos and work on your technique, speed, and power.

Recommended Boxing Bag
