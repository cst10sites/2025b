Beginner Boxing Techniques

1. Stance (How You Stand)

Right-handed? Step forward with your left foot.

Left-handed? Step forward with your right foot.

Keep feet shoulder-width apart. Knees slightly bent. Hands up to protect your face.

Tip: Stay light on your toes, not flat-footed.



2. Footwork (How You Move)

Move forward: Front foot steps, back foot follows.

Move backward: Back foot steps, front foot follows.

Move side to side: Step with the lead foot, then the other.

Tip: Always stay in your stance. Don’t cross your feet.



3. Jab (Quick Front Hand Punch)

Punch straight with your front hand (left for righties).

Snap it out and bring it back fast.

Tip: Keep your back hand by your face.

4. Cross (Strong Back Hand Punch)

Punch with your back hand (right for righties).

Turn your back foot and hips to add power.

Tip: Keep your front hand up to guard.



5. Hook (Side Punch)

Use your front hand to swing sideways.

Elbow stays up and punch comes across.

Tip: Turn your hips slightly for more power.



6. Guard (Protect Yourself)

Always keep your hands up near your cheeks.

Elbows tucked in to block your body.

Tip: This is your shield—never drop it.
