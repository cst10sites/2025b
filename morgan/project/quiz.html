Game Introduction

In this game you will flip over cards and try to make matches with the different animal images. The game highlighting how every interaction with the environment carries weight, whether remembered or not. Continue scrolling down to learn about what you can do to help the hurting ecosytemes.

Helping Animals and the Environment

  • Try to bike or bus as commute instead of driving to reduce fuel emmissions
  • Avoid feeding wild animals. It can be harmful for them because the food could be unhealthy and they could grow dependant on humans
  • Support the community by joining clean-up which will help to prevent animals from eating litter.
  • Reducce, reuse, recycle to reduce waste
