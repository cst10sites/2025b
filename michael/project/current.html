Current State of AI

In the past couple years, AI has advanced as a whole in numerous ways. Sentience is still far away, but AI now can use machine learning to make predictions and learn from it's mistakes. On top of that, many AI chatbots are capable of holding conversation with humans.

Although not sentient, our current AI is already extremely advanced. As of now, AI can display memory, hold human conversation, and stimulate emotions and reasoning. However, it doesn't actually "feel" emotions, but stimulates it when it reasons that it is appropriate. The reasoning is also based off of pre-existing data that is stored, rather than human reasoning. As of now, AI is only advanced enough to mimic sentient behaviour.
