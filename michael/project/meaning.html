What is Sentience?

Sentience is the capacity to fully feel and think independently. It isn't just the ability to move or act but also to be able to perceive, suffer, and enjoy. In other words, sentience is "having a mind of its own," where one can make their own decisions and think completely independently, as well as having a consciousness.


Having a truly sentient/conscious AI would be a gigantic leap beyond our current AI capabilities. A system like that wouldn't just process information but also experience it. AI tools today are already extremely useful, but a sentient AI would be capable of doing it all without needing human prompts. This would make it extremely useful but also extremely dangerous at the same time.
